Hello, I’m Taehyun Lee. I’m a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University. I am advised by Prof. Gunhee Kim as a member of Vision & Learning Lab.

I research to make machines capable of brainstorming creative and reliable ideas. For this goal, I am interested in evaluating and enhancing machines'(LLMs/VLMs) ability of concept combination.
